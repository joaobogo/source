FAQ

Most Common Questions About Our Services

? What is business process outsourcing?

Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) is the practice where companies outsource certain business functions, such as payroll, customer service, and IT management, to focus on more important tasks. The main benefits include cost reduction, operational flexibility, and improved service quality.

? Why choose remote team management instead of collaborating with freelance developers?

Remote team management offers greater availability and security compared to freelancers. Unlike freelancers, remote teams consist of vetted professionals who work within a structured environment, ensuring accountability and adherence to best practices. Remote teams also provide reliable post-development support.

? What roles, talents, and specialists can you provide to enhance our team?

We offer a wide range of specialists including experts in web development, mobile development, cybersecurity, UX/UI design, and cloud development. We also support the telecommunications industry with roles such as customer service, helpdesk support, and sales development representatives (SDRs).

? How do you ensure the quality of the candidates you provide?

Our HR team, with a background in psychology, follows a structured process to select only the best candidates. We ensure that candidates meet required skills, company culture, and any specific client needs, ensuring high-quality candidates for your business.

? How do you handle the onboarding process?

Our onboarding process involves defining project scope, aligning skills and resources, recruiting top talent, and handling administrative tasks like payroll, taxes, and legal requirements. We ensure smooth integration and provide ongoing monitoring to guarantee team efficiency and satisfaction.

? How do you manage and keep remote teams engaged?

At Frontall USA, we focus on creating a seamless connection between your in-house team and the outsourced team. Our remote teams work in your time zone, using the same tools and systems, to ensure engagement and productivity. We emphasize trust, clear communication, and team culture.

? How does Frontall USA support its remote employees?

Frontall USA fosters a supportive work environment with a people-first philosophy. We offer benefits such as health and dental insurance, food vouchers, gym access, and wellness programs to ensure our remote employees feel valued and supported.

? What if I'm not satisfied with the candidates you provide?

If you're not satisfied with a candidate, we offer a 15-day replacement guarantee. After selection and contract signing, if the candidate doesn’t meet expectations, we’ll provide a replacement at no additional cost.
